# ScoutforU Consultants — Recruiter Incentive Plan
**Effective 1 August 2026 · Financial Year 2026–27**

All targets below are **per recruiter**. Closures are counted on the financial
year: Q1 Apr–Jun, Q2 Jul–Sep, Q3 Oct–Dec, Q4 Jan–Mar.

---

## 1. Quarterly Incentive

| Closures in the quarter | Incentive per closure | Milestone bonus |
|---|---|---|
| 1 – 2 | ₹2,000 | — |
| 3 – 4 | ₹3,000 | + ₹3,000 on reaching 4 |
| 5 and above | ₹4,000 | + ₹6,000 on reaching 6 |

The rate is set by the total closures achieved in that quarter and applies to
every closure in it.

**Examples**
- 2 closures → ₹4,000
- 3 closures → ₹9,000
- 4 closures → ₹15,000 (₹12,000 + ₹3,000 bonus)
- 6 closures → ₹30,000 (₹24,000 + ₹6,000 bonus)

---

## 2. Half-Yearly Bonus (cumulative)

H1 = April–September · H2 = October–March

| Closures in the half-year | Bonus |
|---|---|
| 5 – 6 | ₹5,000 |
| 7 – 9 | ₹10,000 |
| 10 and above | ₹18,000 |

**Condition:** at least **2 closures in each quarter** of that half-year.

---

## 3. Annual Bonus (Apr–Mar)

| Closures in the year | Bonus |
|---|---|
| 10 – 13 | ₹15,000 |
| 14 – 18 | ₹30,000 |
| 19 and above | ₹50,000 **+ a domestic trip for 2** |

---

## 4. When does a closure count?

A closure becomes eligible for incentive only when **both** are true:

1. The candidate has **completed 30 days** with the client, and
2. The **client's invoice is fully settled**.

Closures that are cancelled, or where the candidate leaves within the 90-day
replacement guarantee, do not count. If an incentive has already been paid and
the candidate leaves inside the guarantee period, it is adjusted against the
next payout.

## 5. Payout schedule

| Component | Paid |
|---|---|
| Quarterly incentive | With the payroll of the month following quarter-end |
| Half-yearly bonus | With the payroll of the month following the half-year |
| Annual bonus | With the payroll of the month following 31 March |

## 6. Transition for FY 2026–27

This plan takes effect **1 August 2026**. For the current quarter (Jul–Sep 2026),
only closures from **1 August onwards** are counted, and the quarterly milestone
bonus threshold is reduced by one closure (bonus at 3 instead of 4).

Closures before 1 August 2026 remain under the earlier arrangement.

---

*Every recruiter can see their own live closure count, qualifying closures and
running incentive in the ScoutforU ATS under **Performance**. Any dispute should
be raised within 15 days of the payout date.*
